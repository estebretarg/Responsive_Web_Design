Vicente Amigo Girol was born in Guadalcanal, Sevilla province, but he grew up in Cordoba. His first teachers were "El Tomate" and "El Merengue", later he become learner of Manolo Sanlúcar.

Discography:

Vicente's production caracterize for his very deep flamenco roots combined whit a modern and fresh character. Since the very begining he was exploring new ways to express his own voice trough the music.

Albums:

  • De mi corazón al aire (1991)
  • Vivencias imaginadas (1995)
  • Poeta (1997)
  • Ciudad de las ideas (2000)
  • Un momento en el sonido (2004)
  • Paseo de la gracia (2009)
  • Tierra (2013)
  • Memoria de los sentidos (2017)
